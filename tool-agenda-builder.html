How it works

Your topics in. A real agenda out.

No more scrambling the night before. Enter your agenda items and we'll handle the timing, structure, and formatting.

Step 01
📝

List your topics

Enter your agenda items, durations, item types, and owners. Add your school logo and brand colors for a polished look.

Step 02
⚡

Generate instantly

Click Generate and see a formatted, professional agenda document — complete with a numbered table, type badges, and total time.

Step 03
📤

Export and share

Print to PDF, export to Word or PowerPoint, open in Google Docs, or download a print-ready 8.5×11 flyer version.

What's included

Built for how boards actually work.

⏱️

Per-item time tracking

Each agenda item has its own duration. Total meeting time is calculated automatically and shown at the bottom.

🏷️

Item type badges

Color-coded badges for Discussion, Action, Info, Vote, and Break — so everyone knows what's expected at each item.

👤

Owner assignments

Each item shows who's leading it — President, Treasurer, Committee Chair — so there's no confusion in the room.

✏️

Click-to-edit preview

Every element of the generated agenda is editable — click any text on the preview and type directly before exporting.

📄

Multiple export formats

Print, copy text, export to Word (.docx), download a PowerPoint stub, or open directly in Google Docs or Slides.

🖨️

Print-ready 8.5×11 flyer

Download a formatted letter-size flyer version of your agenda — perfect to hand out at the door or post on the school board.
