Common questions

Before you write — check here first.

What does a subscription include?
The Starter plan ($19/mo) gives you 10 AI generations per month, the Flyer Writer, Newsletter Blurb, Permission Slip generator, and 50 directory entries. The Pro Board plan ($24/mo) unlocks all 11 tools with unlimited generations. See the full breakdown on our pricing page.
Is this built for both PTAs and PTOs?
Yes — absolutely both. The PTA/PTO Shoppe works equally well for National PTA-affiliated groups and independent PTOs. Our tools are designed for any parent volunteer organization running a school community.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel anytime from your account settings — no contracts, no cancellation fees. Your access continues through the end of your billing period.
Do you offer school-year billing?
Yes! We offer a school-year billing option (September–June) for boards that don't need the tool over summer. Contact us and we'll set it up for your account.
Is there a District plan for multiple schools?
Yes. Our District plan ($69/mo) covers up to 10 school accounts with a shared admin dashboard, per-school brand settings, and priority support. Contact us for a demo.
What if I need help getting started?
Every new member gets access to our Getting Started guide and quick-start videos. Pro Board and District members get email onboarding support. We're here — just reach out via this form.
