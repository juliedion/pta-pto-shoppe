Example output

An email that opens wallets.

Subject: We're $3,200 away — here's how you can make the difference 💙
From: Jefferson Elementary PTA <pta@jefferson.edu>

Our kids deserve better science equipment — and we're almost there.

Hi Jefferson Families,

Two weeks ago, Mrs. Torres's 4th graders were supposed to do their first microscope lab. But we only have 6 working microscopes for 28 kids. So they shared. And waited. And some didn't get a turn at all.

We're running our Spring Science Fund this month to change that. Our goal is $8,000 for 20 new digital microscopes — and as of today, we've raised $4,800. That means we're $3,200 away.

🎯
$4,800 raised of $8,000 goal · Deadline: April 15

Every $160 funds one microscope. Can you help us get one — or help a family who can't? Any amount helps.
